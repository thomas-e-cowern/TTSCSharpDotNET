Thomas E Cowern

Full Stack and iOS Developer


Hello there, my name is Tom, and I am an iOS Developer specializing in apps that make organizing the things that matter in your life easier. In the military, I spent long periods of time working on systems that were not designed to be user friendly or intuitive (Thanks Uncle Sam!). This drove home the point that if a system isn’t easy to use and if it doesn’t do what the user needs it to do, then it isn’t going to get used unless absolutely necessary. My goal, no matter what project I’m working on, is to make sure the app meets both of those criteria.

I am also an Air Force Veteran, Apple fan boy, motorcyclist, watch aficionado, mechanical typewriter collector, budding artist, reader, world traveler and cook. I just bought a harmonica but have yet to learn how to play it. I am turning my interest in watches and manual typewriters into apps that will be headed to the app store soon.
